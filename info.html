Play strategy

Navigate through labyrinths of mirrors, refracting glass, portals, and obstacles. Your goal is to guide objects using light physics to reach the target. But beware: walls absorb rays, glass alters their trajectory, and some objects activate only under specific conditions! Can you calculate every angle and uncover non-obvious solutions?

  • Mirror

    You can reflect rays using a mirror

  • Wall

    The wall blocks the passage of the rays

  • Glass

    Glass refracts the incoming light beam

  • Portals

    A portal helps move the beam to the desired location

  • Door

    The door can open when you press it
